beef burrito recipe

source 1 source 2

Ingredients:

  • flour tortilla
  • black beans
  • cabbage/lettuce
  • tomato
  • red onion
  • cheese
  • cilantro

Recipe:

  1. cook beef over medium heat
  2. stir in taco seasoning
  3. cook refried beans over medium-low heat
  4. put 1/3 cup beans off-center on each tortilla before topping with 1/4 cup beef. sprinkle cheese
  5. fold up burrito and cook in skillet
