curry recipe

source

Ingredients:

  • yellow curry powder
  • garam masala
  • chicken breast or thighs
  • kosher salt
  • coconut milk
  • olive oil
  • onion
  • jalapeno
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • fresh lemon juice
  • chicken broth
  • cornstarch
  • cilantro

Recipe:

  1. toast spices
  2. chop and season chicken breast
  3. cover chicken with coconut oil and marinate for a few minutes
  4. chop and cook onion, jalapeno, and ginger/garlic
  5. add lemon juice, add chicken in coconut milk, add broth
  6. thicken sauce and simmer. then eat it up!!!
